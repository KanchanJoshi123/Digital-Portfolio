INTERNSHIP

Android developer at Bank Of Maharashtra

Bank of Maharashtra has various zones across India.Zonal managers are required to visit branches under their respective zones.During branch visits they need to capture various information and are required to retrieve as per requirement. As of now reporting is done to the head office using excel sheets and one person has to manually collect all the excel sheets received and a combined report is then provided to top authorities. There is a possibility that there will be human error. So I tried to digitise the process using a mobile application.
